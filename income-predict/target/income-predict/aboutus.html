Hi Folks,

We are providing service for predicting individual's income range based on certain factors. Here we are applying Kmeans clustering on Hadoop MapReduce software framework to predict the income. The motive of the service is to predict the income range of an individual and to provide a market value of a person with respect to various factors such as education, skill set and so on. This is unique service which can be given to the people for knowing their actual market worth and expected salary range.
